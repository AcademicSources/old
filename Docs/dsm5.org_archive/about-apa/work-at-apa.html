Current Job Opportunities

Thank you for your interest in working at the American Psychiatric Association (APA).

  • January 19, 2017

Associate Director of Payment Policy

Reporting to the Director of Reimbursement Policy, the Associate Director of Payment Policy will be responsible for providing expertise, advocacy, and member education regarding payment policies and payment reform under Medicare, Medicare Advantage, and Medicaid. The incumbent will play a leading role on issues regarding the Medicare Quality Payment Program (Merit-Based Incentive Payment System and Advanced Alternative Payment Model Incentives), and other alternative payment models. The Associate Director will work collaboratively with other APA colleagues, departments, and members, as well as outside organizations, in addition to representing the APA before the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS), Medicare Payment Advisory Commission (MedPAC), Medicaid and CHIP Payment and Access Commission (MACPAC), and other relevant federal agencies.

View More

  • January 9, 2017

Senior Meeting Planner

The Senior Meeting Planner is responsible for all logistics for APA Component Meetings (except governance meetings) and Allied Group Meetings at the Annual Meeting and IPS, including developing and maintaining online meeting request forms, selecting menu items, and maintaining the specbook database and for producing the specbook sent to all facilities and vendors.

View More

  • January 4, 2017

Deputy Director, Division of Diversity and Health Equity

The Division of Diversity & Health Equity (DDHE) works to meet the professional needs of psychiatrists from minority and underrepresented groups and improve the quality of care for minority and underserved populations.

The Deputy Director represents the Director and the Division internally and externally. S/he acts in concert with the Director to develop the strategic initiatives of the DDHE and acts in the stead of the Director as needed. Provides expert administration/management for the Division overall and is specifically responsible for budgetary and fiscal administration. Works with the Director to plan, organize and manage all the resources of the Division. The Deputy Director directly supervises the Associate Director and Program Managers (2) in the Division. The Deputy Director will also oversee the APA/APF Fellowship Programs reporting to the Director of DDHE/Director of the APA/APAF Fellowship Program.

View More

  • December 9, 2016

Manager of State Relations

Reporting to the Director of State Affairs, the Manager of State Relations will manage and support advocacy by APA member physicians and performing a variety of tasks in support of the day-to-day management of the State Government Affairs (SGA) function.
