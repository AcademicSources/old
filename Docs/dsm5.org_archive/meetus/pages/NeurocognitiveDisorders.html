Neurocognitive Disorders 

The Neurocognitive Disorders Work Group was chaired by Dilip V. Jeste, M.D. until he was elected as APA President-Elect in early 2011. The Work Group is now chaired by Dan M. Blazer, M.D., Ph.D., MPH and co-chaired Ronald Petersen, Ph.D., M.D. Dilip V. Jeste, M.D. now serves as Chair Emeritus. Its members and disorders of study are listed below.
