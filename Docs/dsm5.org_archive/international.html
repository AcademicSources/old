International Members

Psychiatrists residing outside the United States and Canada can become an International Member of the APA. As an International Member, you become part of a psychiatric membership organization with 36,000 psychiatrists in over 100 countries. International members gain access to multiple opportunities to enhance their professional growth as part of a global community of psychiatric physicians at the forefront of advancements in psychiatry and medicine.

Join Now

Download Application

Benefits and Services

Publications

  • Receive the latest news in your field
    • Free online subscriptions to American Journal of Psychiatry and Psychiatric News, with articles on cutting-edge research and updating you on developments throughout the field ($288 value)
    • Discounted subscription to Psychiatric Services, APA's monthly journal focusing on the delivery of behavioral health services in organized systems ($22 value)
  • 20% discount on over 700 books and special member pricing for journals and subscriptions through APA Publishing
  • Member discounted rate to DSM-5 eBook and 300 other titles to build your professional library

e-Learning

  • Access to FREE online educational modules to advance clinical and professional competencies. Earn continuing medical education (CME) credits and continued professional development (CPD) credits (over $500 value)
  • Try the APA Learning Center free with the Members’ Course of the Month. Each month members have free access to an on-demand CME course on a popular topic.

Live Learning

  • Registration discounts to APA Annual Meeting ($600 value) and IPS: The Mental Health Services Conference ($190 value)

Additional Benefits

  • Access to the online international and US membership directory
  • Privileged access to "members only" content on the APA website
  • Subscriptions to online newsletters: Psychiatric News Update and Integrated Care Newsletter.
  • Strong advocacy on the international level on issues affecting patients and psychiatrists around the world

2016 APA Dues Rates for International Members

As a new international member your first year APA dues are discounted by 25% and based on the income group category of your country of residence as defined by the World Bank.

View income groups of countries in alphabetical order .

Dues Amount per World Bank Income Group

  • Low Income
    • $50.00 USD
    • $37.50 USD (Dues After 25% Discount)
  • Lower Middle Income
    • $130.00 USD
    • $97.50 USD (Dues After 25% Discount)
  • Upper Middle Income
    • $180.00 USD
    • $135.00 USD (Dues After 25% Discount)
  • High Income
    • $210.00 USD
    • $157.50 USD (Dues After 25% Discount)

You can pay your dues:

  • Via Fax, using the Fax Payment Form for International Membership Dues, to Membership fax +1-703-907-1085
  • By Phone, at +1-703-907-7300, Monday-Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. ET

Contact Your Membership Coordinator

Please contact the Membership Coordinator for your region regarding any questions about your membership account and/or payments.
