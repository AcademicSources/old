Medical Students

Medical students enrolled in a U.S. or Canadian school of medicine, including osteopathic medicine are eligible to join the APA as a Medical Student Member.

Discover your career in psychiatry. Learn more about psychiatry through a complimentary membership to the APA. Even if psychiatry is not your chosen specialty, mental health issues correlate to many other areas of practice. As a medical student member you can start building lifelong professional relationships and tap into APA’s wealth of research and practical information.

Benefits and Services

Publications

  • Receive the latest news in your field
    • Free online subscription to American Journal of Psychiatry and Psychiatric News, with articles on cutting-edge research and updating you on developments through the field ($288 value)
  • 20% discount to over 700 books and special member pricing for journals and subscriptions through APA Publishing

Live Learning

  • Free registration to APA Annual Meeting ($1,080 value) and IPS: The Mental Health Services Conference ($590 value)
  • Resident and medical student poster competition provides opportunities to share your research projects and practice experiments at the Annual Meeting ($580 value)

Career Development

  • Access to online directory of summer jobs and fellowship opportunities
  • Networking and professional development opportunities through APA’s social medial presence on LinkedIn, Facebook and Twitter and through special APA listservs

Additional Benefits

  • Mentoring networks and career development sessions at the Annual Meeting
  • Online resources including What is Psychiatry?, Guide for Applying for Psychiatry Residency, Building a Career in Psychiatry, and Resident’s Guide to Surviving Training

Free Membership for Medical Students

Medical student members are dues exempt and belong to APA at the national level only (there are no dual membership requirements with the local District Branch/State Association).
