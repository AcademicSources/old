DSM-5 Personality Assessment Formats 

  Option 1:

LEVEL OF PERSONALITY FUNCTIONING

Indicate the level of personality functioning that most closely characterizes the patient:

 _____  0 - No Impairment

 _____  1 - Mild Impairment

 _____  2 - Moderate Impairment

 _____  3 - Serious Impairment

 _____  4 - Extreme Impairment

 

TYPE MATCH PROFILE

Indicate the degree to which the patient matches each of the following types:

Antisocial/Psychopathic:        

5 (very good)     4 (good)        3 (moderate)       2 (slight)        1 (no)

Avoidant:                             

5 (very good)     4 (good)        3 (moderate)       2 (slight)        1 (no)

Borderline:                            

5 (very good)     4 (good)        3 (moderate)       2 (slight)        1 (no)

Obsessive-Compulsive:         

5 (very good)     4 (good)        3 (moderate)       2 (slight)        1 (no)

Schizotypal:                          

5 (very good)     4 (good)        3 (moderate)       2 (slight)        1 (no)
