292.9
Inhalant-Related Disorder NOS

  • Proposed  Revision
  • Rationale
  • Severity
  • DSM-IV
 

No revisions are being recommended for this disorder at the current time. 

 

No revisions are being recommended for this disorder at the current time. 

 

Recommendations for severity criteria for this disorder are forthcoming.  We encourage you to check our Web site regularly for updates.

Inhalant-Disorder Not Otherwise Specified

The Inhalant-Related Disorder Not Otherwise Specified category is for disorders associated with the use of inhalants that are not classifiable as one of the disorders listed above.
