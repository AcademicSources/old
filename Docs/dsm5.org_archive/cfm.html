DSM-V Prelude Project: Research and Outreach

Welcome to the DSM-V Prelude Project. The American Psychiatric Association has created this web site in order to keep the public and professionals informed about the plans for DSM-V as well as the ongoing effort to enrich the research base as formal work on DSM-V begins. In addition, this web site provides an opportunity for you to alert us to problems in the DSM-IV that you may have encountered and to provide your suggestions for DSM-V. Information about the current 4th edition (text revision) of the Diagnostic and Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders (DSM-IV-TR) is available on the the DSM-IV-TR web site.
