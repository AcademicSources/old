Publications from DSM-5 Development 

 As part of the efforts to make information about DSM-5 development as widely disseminated as possible, the American Psychiatric Institute for Research and Education is maintaining an ongoing list of peer-reviewed journal publications arising from the DSM-5 planning conference series (2002-08) and from DSM-5 Task Force and Work Group discussions (e.g., empirical literature reviews, secondary data analyses). This list will be continually updated.
